================================================================================
DIEM MANH
================================================================================

1. CHI PHI: 9/10
   - Chi phi phan cung: 0.95-1.15 trieu dong (< 2 trieu dieu kien)
   - Co the tim tai tro tu nha truong / cuoc thi
   - ROI cao cho nong thon (< 100K/ho neu chia se)

2. CONG NGHE: 8.5/10
   - Toan bo linh kien tieu chuan, mua duoc tren thi truong Viet Nam
   - Code Arduino C voi thu vien chuan, co cong dong ho tro
   - So do ket noi ro rang, huong dan chi tiet
   - Breadboard + day jumper, khong can han linh kien

3. NANG LUONG: 8/10
   - Pin 18650 (2x): du cho cuoc thi (demo 1-2 ngay)
   - Solar Panel 15W: 60 Wh/ngay ≈ du nhu cau 72 Wh (troi nang)
   - Thoi gian chay khong nang: 5-7 ngay (voi Solar)
   - Phu hop vung nong thon khong co dien luoi

4. THUC TIEN: 8.5/10
   - Thoi gian lap rap: 4-6 gio (hoc sinh cap 3 co huong dan)
   - Khong can phan mem DAC biet, chi can Arduino IDE + thu vien
   - SMS canh bao khong can Internet
   - Khong can dien luoi cong cong

5. GIAO DUC: 9/10
   - Vat ly: quang dien, sieu am, mach dien
   - Ky thuat: cam bien, giao tiep I2C/UART, nang luong
   - Lap trinh: C, Arduino, xu ly du lieu
   - Toan: tinh toan nang luong, loc nhieu

6. XA HOI: 9.5/10
   - Giai quyet lu lut: 2/3 dien tich Viet Nam
   - Bao ve hang chuc ngan nguoi neu trien khai
   - Su dung nang luong tai tao (ben vung)
   - Giam canh bao som giup dan so

7. NANG CAP: 9/10
   - Lo trinh ro rang (10 tinh nang cai thien)
   - Khong be tac cong nghe
   - Co the nam rong tu demo -> san xuat hang loat

================================================================================
HAN CHE
================================================================================

1. THOI GIAN CHAY NGAN (pin 18650)
   - Chi 1-2 ngay -> can nang cap Solar + pin 10Ah cho ung dung dai han
   - Giai phap: nang cap Solar Panel 15W + pin LiFePO4 10Ah

2. DO CHINH XAC CAM BIEN
   - SRF04 bi anh huong boi do am, nuoc, vat chuong ngai
   - Giai phap: tru binh dong, loc nhieu, them cam bien backup (ap suat)

3. QUAN LY NANG LUONG
   - Can huong dan them cho hoc sinh (hysteresis, EEPROM)
   - Can calibrate, hieu chinh nguong theo mua

4. CHI PHI SIM HANG THANG
   - +50-100K/thang -> tong 3 nam ~1.8M
   - Can tinh toan chi phi tang them

================================================================================
TINH KHA THI TUNG KHIA CANH
================================================================================

A. CONG NGHE: 8.5/10

Diem Manh:
  + Linh kien tieu chuan, pho bien, hieu ung
  + Arduino UNO R3 pho bien, co thu vien hang can (RTClib, Wire, SoftwareSerial)
  + Breadboard + day jumper -> khong can han linh kien
  + So do ket noi chi tiet, huong dan rõ rang
  + Nhieu lua chon thay the (SIM800L, DS1307, DHT22)

Tham Tho:
  + Cam bien SRF04: anh huong am do, noi + tat lien tuc
  + Module SIM: can cau hinh AT Command phuc tap
  + Calibrate: hoc sinh can huong dan them

B. KINH TE: 9/10

Chi Phi:
  - Arduino UNO R3:        150K
  - Cam bien (SRF04, RTC):  85K
  - Hien thi (LCD, Buzzer): 115K
  - Giao tiep (SIM):        145K
  - Nang luong (pin):       115K
  - Phu kien (day):          55K
  - ---- TONG ----          665K (can nen)
  - Solar (tuy chon):       150K
  - ---- TONG DAY DU ----   815-965K

Nhan Xet:
  + Chi phi < 1 trieu -> thap hon nhieu so voi he thong chuyen nghiep (100M+)
  + Co the tim tai tro, hoc sinh co the mua duoc
  + ROI cao neu trien khai (1 trieu bao ve nhieu ho dan)
  - Chi phi SIM them: 50-100K/thang -> 600-1200K/nam

C. NANG LUONG: 8/10

Tieu Thu:
  - Binh thuong: 170-260 mA x 24h = 4-6.2 Ah/ngay
  - Co gui SMS: +0.5-1 Ah = 6-7 Ah/ngay
  - Peak: 900 mA khi canh bao

Phuong An 1 - Pin 18650 (2x, 5Ah):
  + Don gian, chi phi thap (115K)
  - Thoi gian chay: 1-2 ngay (khong dat yeu cau 24/7)
  + Phu hop demo / cuoc thi

Phuong An 2 - Solar Panel 15W:
  + Sinh ra: 60 Wh/ngay (troi nang 4h) ≈ du nhu cau 72 Wh
  + Thoi gian chay: 5-7 ngay khong nang
  - Phu thuoc thoi tiet
  + Chi phi them: 150K (chap nhan duoc)

D. THUC TIEN: 8.5/10

Lap Rap:
  + Thoi gian: 4-6 gio / nhom 2-3 hoc sinh cap 3
  + Khong can han, chi can breadboard + day jumper
  + Co serial monitor, LCD debug -> de debug

Trien Khai:
  + Khong can dien luoi cong cong
  + Module SIM A7680C -> gui SMS den dien thoai dan so
  + Co the lap trong hop plastic chong tham, dat ngoai troi
  - Can kiem tra dinh ky & hieu chinh nguong

Kho Khan:
  - Chon vi tri cam bien (cao hon muc nuoc toi da)
  - Bao ve chong nuoc (hop/vo phai chong nuoc hoan toan)
  - Quan ly cap dien (can che phu, giu kin)

E. GIAO DUC: 9/10

Vat Ly:
  - Hieu ung quang dien (photovoltaic)
  - Sieu am (ultrasound)
  - Mach dien + Nguon nang luong

Ky Thuat:
  - Cam bien sieu am (dap ung)
  - Giao tiep I2C (LCD + RTC)
  - Giao tiep UART (Arduino + SIM)
  - Quan ly nang luong

Lap Trinh:
  - Vong lap, mang, ham
  - Xu ly chuoi du lieu
  - Luu tru EEPROM
  - Hysteresis logic

Toan:
  - Tinh khoang cach = (thoi gian x van toc) / 2
  - Chuyen doi ADC -> Dien ap
  - Tinh toan nang luong (P = U x I)

F. XA HOI: 9.5/10

Giai Quyet Van De:
  - Lu lut anh huong 2/3 dien tich Viet Nam
  - Giam thiet hai -> bao ve dan so
  - Canh bao som giup son tru duoc tro cap

Tac Dong Dua Hoc Sinh:
  - Hoc sinh tu thiet ke, lap rap, kiem tra
  - Phat trien ky nang STEM
  - Co hoi nang cao suc khoe

Tinh Ben Vung:
  - Su dung nang luong tai tao (mat troi)
  - Chi phi thap -> pho cap rong
  - Khong phu thuoc dien luoi
